Tournament Opportunities with WHCFC

If you try out and make the team, you will develop a sense of belonging and will enjoy:

  • Tournaments in 2025: The first one being in May; these activities for WHCFC team members are fun but competitive. They require commitment, such as attending practices separate from Free & Fun Scrimmages. Players should be teachable, want to develop teamwork, play competitively, and learn play patterns, set pieces while getting fit and enhancing their soccer skills.
  • Affordable Tournament Costs: Our value proposition is to provide free football opportunities. Therefore, tournament costs (entry fees, team uniforms, etc.) will be kept low and affordable, especially for those in equity-deserving and marginalized groups. We will consider what players can realistically pay and work with sponsors to fund the program.
  • How to Get Involved: First, come out in April 2025 for Free, Fun Football Scrimmages. Then express your interest in try-outs, and if you make the WHCFC team, you will participate in tournaments.

Should you require further details or want to show your interest in playing Free, Fun Football Scrimmages or in Tournaments & Friendlies, email fcwhitehaven@gmail.com.
